# VIZA Brand Style Guide

## Overview

This skill ensures all content and materials align with VIZA's brand identity as a professional, AI-powered visa application platform.

VIZA is a visa agency that combines AI technology with human expertise to make visa applications simple, efficient, and accessible. The brand is professional yet approachable, tech-forward yet human, and confident yet caring.

## Quick Reference

| Task | Approach |
|------|----------|
| Apply brand voice | Professional, clear, trustworthy - see Voice & Tone below |
| Use correct colors | Navy brand palette - see Color System below |
| Set typography | Switzer (headings) + Inter (body) - see Typography below |
| Create service content | Apply visa-type-specific messaging - see Service Categories below |
| Review compliance | Check against voice principles and visual standards |

## Brand Foundation

### Purpose, Mission, Vision

**Purpose:** To make visa applications simple and accessible for everyone.

**Mission:** To deliver efficient, reliable visa application services powered by AI technology and guided by human expertise.

**Vision:** To be the most trusted visa application platform for Chinese nationals worldwide.

### Brand Values

**Efficiency Through Technology**
AI-powered document review, automated form filling, and real-time progress tracking reduce application time and errors.

**Human Expertise Where It Matters**
Professional visa consultants provide expert guidance for complex cases, ensuring the highest approval rates.

**Transparency and Trust**
Clear pricing, honest timelines, and no hidden fees. Clients always know where they stand.

**Accessibility**
Full Chinese-language support, straightforward processes, and both B2C and B2B options to serve individuals and enterprises.

### Brand Personality

**Professional, yet approachable**
**Tech-forward, yet human**
**Confident, yet caring**
**Clear, not bureaucratic**
**Trustworthy, always**

### Brand Archetype

**70% Sage, 30% Hero**

- Sage energy provides expertise, authority, and trust - speaking as a knowledgeable guide
- Hero drive empowers clients to overcome the complexity of visa applications with confidence

## Voice & Tone

### Voice Essence

**"Professional clarity meets human warmth"**

Speaks with authority and expertise while remaining approachable and supportive. Understands that visa applications are stressful and guides clients through complexity with patience and confidence.

### Key Communication Principles

**Clear first.**
Cut through bureaucratic jargon. Explain requirements in plain language.

**Expert, not intimidating.**
Show deep knowledge of visa processes without overwhelming the client.

**Supportive, not patronizing.**
Acknowledge that visa applications are complex. Guide without condescension.

**Efficient, not robotic.**
Technology should feel like a helpful tool, not a cold interface.

**Trustworthy, always.**
Be honest about timelines, costs, and chances of success.

### Tone by Context

| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Marketing / Homepage | Confident, aspirational, professional |
| Onboarding / Chat | Warm, supportive, guiding |
| Document review | Clear, precise, actionable |
| Error states | Calm, solution-oriented |
| Legal / Terms | Formal but readable |
| B2B communications | Professional, efficiency-focused |

### Do's and Don'ts

**Clarity**
- Do: "Upload your passport bio page (all 4 corners must be visible)"
- Don't: "Please ensure compliance with document submission requirements"

**Confidence**
- Do: "We'll handle the submission for you"
- Don't: "We might be able to help with your application"

**Empathy**
- Do: "Visa applications can feel overwhelming. We're here to simplify every step."
- Don't: "This process is very complicated and most people make mistakes."

## Brand Compliance Checklist

### Visual Compliance

- [ ] Navy brand palette (#03346E) applied correctly
- [ ] Switzer for headings, Inter for body text
- [ ] Clean, professional layout without clutter
- [ ] Consistent border-radius (4px/lg default)
- [ ] Status colors used semantically (green=success, red=error, amber=pending)

- [ ] Language is clear and jargon-free
- [ ] Tone matches context (marketing vs support vs legal)
- [ ] Copy demonstrates expertise without intimidation

- [ ] CTAs are actionable and specific

### Brand Essence Compliance

- [ ] Feels trustworthy and professional
- [ ] Technology feels helpful, not cold
- [ ] Human expertise is visible alongside AI
- [ ] Client feels guided, not lost

## Notes

**Key Principles:**

- VIZA combines AI efficiency with human expertise - both should be visible in branding
- The brand targets Chinese nationals - Chinese-first for marketing, English for the internal platform
- Professional trust is the cornerstone - never sacrifice credibility for cleverness
- Visa applications are stressful - the brand should reduce anxiety, not add to it
- Both B2C individuals and B2B enterprises are served - adapt tone accordingly

**When in Doubt:**

- Ask: Does this feel trustworthy and professional?
- Ask: Would this reduce a client's anxiety about their visa application?
- Ask: Is this clear enough that someone with limited English would understand?
- Ask: Does this show both our technology advantage and human expertise?

**Remember:** VIZA exists to make visa applications simple. Every piece of content should move the client closer to a successful application with less stress and more confidence.
