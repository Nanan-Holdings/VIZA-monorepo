# Australia Visitor Visa (Subclass 600) — FAQ

## Documents required

- Passport valid at least 6 months past the intended date of entry.
- Photo 35×45 mm (413×531 px), plain light background.
- Health declaration (TB-screening certificate for stays > 6
  months, or for applicants from a TB-prevalent country).
- Bank statements last 3 months.
- Travel itinerary (flights + accommodation).
- Sponsor / inviter declaration if applying under the Sponsored
  Family stream.

## Processing time

- VIZA prefill of ImmiAccount form: ~1 hour after payment.
- Department of Home Affairs decision: typically 27 days for the
  Tourist stream, longer for Sponsored Family.
- Apply at least 6 weeks before travel.

## Refund eligibility

- Agency fee fully refundable until VIZA reaches the ImmiAccount
  payment screen.
- The visa application charge (~AUD 190 for Tourist stream) is
  paid by the applicant on the portal — non-refundable per Home
  Affairs policy.

## Common rejection reasons

- Insufficient evidence of intention to return (especially for
  applicants under 30 with no clear ties).
- Health declaration missing or expired (TB cert valid 6 months).
- Sponsored Family declarations missing the sponsor's signature
  or supporting documents.
