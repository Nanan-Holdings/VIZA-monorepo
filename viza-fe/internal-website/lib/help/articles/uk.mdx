# UK Standard Visitor (UK_STANDARD_VISITOR) — FAQ

## Documents required

- Passport valid for the entire duration of your stay.
- Photo 35×45 mm (413×531 px @ 300 dpi), light grey or cream
  background.
- Bank statements covering the last 6 months.
- Employment letter (if employed) or business registration (if
  self-employed).
- Travel itinerary (flights + accommodation booking).
- Sponsor details if visiting family / friends.

## Processing time

- VIZA submission to UKVI portal: ~45 minutes after payment clears.
- UKVI standard processing: 3 weeks from biometrics enrolment.
  Priority service (5 working days) and Super-Priority (1 working
  day) cost extra and must be selected on the portal.
- Apply at least 4 weeks before travel for the standard service.

## Refund eligibility

- Agency fee fully refundable until VIZA submits the application
  to UKVI.
- Government fee (~£127 standard, more for priority) is collected
  by UKVI on the portal — VIZA does not handle it. UKVI's refund
  policy applies (typically non-refundable).

## Common rejection reasons

- Insufficient ties to home country.
- Inconsistent travel history (frequent UK trips of long duration).
- Bank statements showing pattern of large pre-application deposits.
- Misalignment between stated purpose and supporting evidence.
