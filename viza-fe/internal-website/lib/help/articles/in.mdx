# India e-Visa (IN_E_VISA) — FAQ

## Documents required

- Passport with at least 2 blank pages and 6 months validity from
  date of arrival.
- Photo 51×51 mm (600×600 px), white background, taken in the
  last 6 months.
- Travel itinerary (flights), Indian inviter / hotel address.
- For e-Medical / e-Conference variants: hospital invitation /
  conference clearance from the Indian Ministry of External
  Affairs.

## Processing time

- VIZA submission via indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa: ~30 minutes.
- Government processing: typically 3–5 working days for e-Tourist;
  longer for Medical / Conference.
- Apply at least 1 week before travel.

## Refund eligibility

- Agency fee fully refundable until VIZA submits to the portal.
- Government e-Visa fee (USD 25–80 depending on variant) is non-
  refundable once the portal accepts the application.

## Common rejection reasons

- Restricted to Ordinary passport holders — Diplomatic / Service
  passports are out of scope.
- Photo dimensions wrong — India insists on 51×51 mm specifically.
- SAARC nationality declaration missing in step 5 (mandatory for
  applicants holding SAARC member-state nationality).
- Ineligible category — Regular Tourist / Employment / Student
  visas go through the consular paper channel, not e-Visa.
