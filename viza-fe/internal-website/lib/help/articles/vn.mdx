# Vietnam e-Visa (VN_E_VISA) — FAQ

## Documents required

- Coloured digital photo, 4×6 cm (472×709 px @ 300 dpi), white
  background, taken in the last 6 months.
- Digital scan of the passport bio page (JPEG, < 1 MB).
- Itinerary if you have one — entry / exit airport plus dates of
  arrival.

## Processing time

- Government processing: typically 3 working days, occasionally up
  to 5.
- VIZA submission: usually under 30 minutes once payment clears.
- We recommend applying at least **2 weeks** before travel.

## Refund eligibility

- Agency fee fully refundable until VIZA's runner starts the
  portal submission.
- Government fee (~USD 25 single-entry, ~USD 50 multi-entry) is
  non-refundable once submitted to the Vietnam Immigration
  Department.

## Common rejection reasons

- Photo background not white, or photo cropped too tight to face.
- Passport scan unreadable / glare on MRZ — re-scan in good light.
- Country of nationality not eligible — see
  https://evisa.xuatnhapcanh.gov.vn/ for the live list.
- Passport expires < 6 months from intended entry.
