Error-message templates
Don't: "Oops! Something went wrong 😬 Please try again later."
Do: "We couldn't save your changes. Check your connection and try again." Include a retry action when possible.
Don't: "Error 422: Invalid date format"
Do: "Please enter your birthday as YYYY-MM-DD." Always tell the user what to do next.
Country & visa naming
Use the full English name: "Indonesia · B211A Business", "UK Standard Visitor Visa", "Japan Tourist Visa".
For zh-CN: "印度尼西亚 · B211A 商务签证", "英国标准访客签证". Never abbreviate country names.
