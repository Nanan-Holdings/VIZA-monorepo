Principles

Only transform and opacity. Never animate width, height, top, or left — they trigger layout and look cheap.   Never exceed 600ms. Users wait on us; we don't wait on our animations.   No scale on press. Use color-only depression (one step darker in the brand scale).   Stagger with delay: 0.1 × index for lists up to ~6 items. Beyond that, animate as one block.   Respect prefers-reduced-motion. Fall back to opacity-only fades at 200ms or skip entirely.
