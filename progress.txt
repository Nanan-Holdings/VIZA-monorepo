  1. The binary-PDF read path (WebFetch saves to tool-results/, then Read with `pages` parameter) is the canonical way to ingest government forms from inside this harness — WebFetch's HTML-only text extraction returns nothing useful on binary PDFs. Worth noting in the playbook as the preferred research-fallback flow.
  2. EU-level forms drift faster than expected — the 2019/1155 amendment (Feb 2020 in force) removed a whole repeatable field and added UK Withdrawal Agreement language.
