# United Kingdom — UK GDPR + DPA 2018

> **Status:** largely compliant. UK IDTA paperwork pending; ICO
> registration open.

## Applicability

- VIZA processes data of UK residents applying for a UK Standard
  Visitor visa (apply-uk-visa.service.gov.uk integration), as well as
  data of UK residents applying for visas to other destinations.
- UK GDPR (UK GDPR + DPA 2018) applies to processing in the UK or
  related to offering services to UK data subjects.
