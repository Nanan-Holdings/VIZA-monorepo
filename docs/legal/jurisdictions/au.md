# Australia — Privacy Act 1988 + APPs

> **Status:** largely compliant. APP 8 cross-border review pending.

## Applicability

VIZA processes Australian residents' data through the AU Subclass 600
runner and any other Australia-bound applications. Under Privacy Act
§5B, the Act applies to organisations "with an Australian link",
which includes carrying on business in Australia.

## Key obligations

| Obligation | VIZA posture |
|---|---|
| APPs 1–13 | Privacy Policy mirrors APP 1; collection notices align with APP 5. |
| APP 8 — cross-border disclosure | We must take "reasonable steps" so the recipient handles data per APPs. SCC equivalents in subprocessor DPAs cover this. |
| Notifiable Data Breaches (Part IIIC) | 30-day assessment window; notify OAIC and affected individuals on serious harm. |
| 2024 amendments (statutory tort, transparency obligations) | Counsel TODO — recent amendments add direct rights of action; review before public launch. |
