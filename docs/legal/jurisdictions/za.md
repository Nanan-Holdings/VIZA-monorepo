# South Africa — POPIA

> **Status:** POPIA-aligned. Information Officer designation
> pending.

## Applicability

VIZA handles South African residents' data through the ZA Visitor
Visa runner and inbound applications. POPIA applies to processing of
personal information of South African data subjects.

## Key obligations

| Obligation | VIZA posture |
|---|---|
| 8 conditions for lawful processing | Privacy Policy maps to POPIA conditions 1–8. |
| Information Officer (S.55) | Required. **Open:** designate + register with the Information Regulator. |
| Trans-border information flow (S.72) | Adequate-protection or contract-based safeguards. SCC equivalents in subprocessor DPAs cover this. |
| Special personal information (children, biometrics) | Where present (passport photo = biometric per some readings), explicit consent required and is captured at signup. |
| Notification of compromise (S.22) | "As soon as reasonably possible" to Regulator + affected data subjects. |

## Gaps

- Information Officer designation + registration.
- POPIA-specific PAIA manual under the Promotion of Access to Information Act.
