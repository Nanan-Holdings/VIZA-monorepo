# Thailand — PDPA (B.E. 2562 / 2019)

> **Status:** PDPA-aligned. DPO designation pending.

## Applicability

VIZA handles Thai residents' data through the TH Tourist e-Visa runner
and inbound applications. PDPA applies to processing of personal data
of individuals in Thailand and to offshore controllers offering
goods or services to Thai data subjects.

## Key obligations

| Obligation | VIZA posture |
|---|---|
| Lawful basis + consent | Captured. PDPA requires explicit, separately granted consent for sensitive data and marketing. |
| DPO (S.41) | Required for "large-scale" or sensitive processing. Counsel TODO. |
| Local representative (S.37) | Required for offshore controllers; designate before public launch. |
| Cross-border transfer (S.28) | Adequate protection or specified safeguards (binding corporate rules, ad-hoc contracts). |
| Breach notification | 72 hours to PDPC. |
