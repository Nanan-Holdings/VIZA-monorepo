# New Zealand — Privacy Act 2020

> **Status:** largely compliant.

## Applicability

VIZA handles New Zealand residents' data through the NZ Visitor Visa
runner and inbound applications. The Privacy Act 2020 applies to
agencies that "carry on business" in New Zealand.

## Key obligations

| Obligation | VIZA posture |
|---|---|
| 13 Information Privacy Principles | Privacy Policy maps to IPPs. |
| Notifiable Privacy Breach (Part 6) | Notify the Privacy Commissioner + affected individuals when serious harm is likely. |
| Cross-border disclosure (IPP 12) | Receiving country/entity must have comparable safeguards or the individual must consent. SCC-equivalents in subprocessor DPAs cover this. |
