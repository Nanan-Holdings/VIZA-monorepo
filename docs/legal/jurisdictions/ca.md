# Canada — PIPEDA + provincial laws

> **Status:** largely compliant under PIPEDA. Quebec Law 25 review
> pending.

## Applicability

VIZA handles Canadian residents' data through the CA TRV runner and
Canada-destination applications. Federally, PIPEDA applies; provincially,
Quebec's Act respecting the protection of personal information in the
private sector ("Law 25", in force 2022–2024 phases) imposes stricter
obligations on Quebec-resident data.

## Key obligations

| Obligation | VIZA posture |
|---|---|
| PIPEDA Fair Information Principles | Privacy Policy maps to all 10 principles. |
| Breach notification (PIPEDA §10.1) | RROSH threshold; notify OPC + affected individuals + record-keeping for 24 months. |
| Quebec Law 25 — Privacy Officer (Art. 3.1) | Required. Designate before serving Quebec residents at scale. |
| Quebec Law 25 — Privacy Impact Assessment (Art. 3.3) | Required before transferring outside Quebec; counsel TODO. |
