# Japan — APPI

> **Status:** largely compliant. APPI joint-disclosure paperwork
> pending.

## Applicability

VIZA handles Japanese residents' data through the JP tourist runner
and inbound applications. APPI (amended 2022, 2023) applies to
processing of personal information of Japanese residents.

## Key obligations

| Obligation | VIZA posture |
|---|---|
| Purpose specification + consent | Captured at signup. |
| Cross-border transfer (APPI Art. 28) | Disclosure of recipient country + protection regime required. Privacy Policy §6 documents Singapore + global subprocessors; counsel TODO to confirm wording. |
| Joint use disclosure (Art. 27) | Where we share with subprocessors that act as joint users (none today), we must publish the joint-use notice. |
| Breach notification | PPC + affected individuals; thresholds in the 2022 amendments. |

## Gaps

- Counsel review of "anonymously processed information" categories
