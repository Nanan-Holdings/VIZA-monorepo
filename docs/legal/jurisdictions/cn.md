# China (PRC) — PIPL / DSL / CSL

> **Status:** not currently applicable. VIZA does not operate in
> mainland China.

## Note

VIZA's runners reach destination portals in jurisdictions that
include some Chinese consular intermediaries (Italy VFS-CN), but
VIZA itself does not collect personal data of mainland-PRC residents
through those flows — applicants who use VIZA to apply for
non-Chinese visas are typically resident in HK / SG / other markets.

If VIZA later offers a Chinese-resident-targeted service (e.g. a
mainland sign-up funnel, or processing data of mainland residents in
mainland China), we would need to:

- Comply with the Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL), the
  Data Security Law (DSL), and the Cybersecurity Law (CSL).
- Register with CAC for cross-border data transfers above relevant
  thresholds and / or sign the Standard Contract for Cross-Border
  Transfer of Personal Information.
- Designate a domestic representative.

Until then this addendum is informational. Counsel should re-review
when the company expands into a mainland-China-targeted offering.
