# Indonesia — UU PDP (Law 27/2022)

> **Status:** transition phase. Counsel review required when full
> implementing regulations enter into force.

## Applicability

VIZA handles Indonesian residents' data through the Indonesia B211a
runner and inbound applications. UU PDP (the Personal Data Protection
Law, Law No. 27/2022) is the umbrella statute; full enforcement
begins on a phased timetable with implementing regulations still
being issued.

## Key obligations

| Obligation | VIZA posture |
|---|---|
| Lawful basis + consent | Captured. |
| Data Protection Officer | Required for "large-scale" processing or sensitive data; threshold criteria in implementing rules. |
| Cross-border transfer | Permitted to jurisdictions with comparable protections or via binding agreements. Singapore + EU subprocessors qualify. |
| Breach notification | 72 hours to the data subject + supervisory authority. |
| Sensitive personal data | Includes biometrics (passport photo) and travel data; stricter consent. |

## Gaps

- Designate DPO once "large-scale" or "sensitive" criteria trigger.
