## 1. Scope of service

VIZA is an AI-assisted visa-application service. The platform:

- Collects applicant information through guided forms and AI-driven
  conversation (the "Companion").
- Translates and normalises that information into the format expected by
  each destination country's visa portal.
- Submits the completed application to the relevant government portal on
  the applicant's behalf, where the portal supports automated
  submission. Where it does not, VIZA delivers a print-ready,
  signed-by-the-applicant submission packet.
- Holds an inbox alias on the applicant's behalf at the haggstorm.com
  domain to receive portal correspondence (OTPs, confirmations).

VIZA is **not** a law firm and does **not** provide immigration legal
advice. No solicitor–client or attorney–client relationship is formed
by using the service. Where local law requires registered immigration
representation (e.g. OISC in the UK, MARA in Australia), VIZA will
either work with a registered partner or decline the engagement, at
VIZA's discretion.

## 2. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and legally able to enter into a
contract in your jurisdiction. Applications on behalf of minors must be
submitted by a parent or legal guardian and will be flagged as such.

## 3. Your responsibilities

- You will provide accurate, complete, and truthful information.
- You will retain the original of any document you upload (passport
  scans, photos, supporting evidence).
- You authorise VIZA to submit data to the destination country's
  government portal on your behalf only after you have ticked the
  per-application authorisation box (LEGAL-002).
- You will respond to requests for additional information within the
  service's stated SLA. Failure to respond may delay or terminate the
  application without refund.

## 4. Fees and payment

- The agency fee is collected up front via Stripe Checkout (PAY-002).
- The government fee for the destination country is collected
  separately (where the portal accepts third-party payment) or paid
  directly by the applicant on the portal (where it does not).
- Payment is in the currency displayed at checkout. Stripe applies
  applicable taxes and currency conversion at the time of charge.

## 5. Refund policy

- The agency fee is **fully refundable** before VIZA's automation has
  initiated portal submission.
- Once submission has started, the agency fee is non-refundable. The
  government fee follows the destination portal's own refund stance,
  which is typically non-refundable on submission.
- A submission that fails for reasons attributable to VIZA's
  automation (i.e. not an applicant data error and not a portal
  outage) is eligible for a re-attempt at no additional charge or, at
  VIZA's option, a full refund of the agency fee.

## 6. No guarantee of outcome

VIZA does not guarantee that any government will issue a visa. The
decision lies with the destination country's authorities. VIZA's
service stops at submission; we do not appeal refusals on your behalf
unless explicitly contracted as a separate engagement.

## 7. Limitation of liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, VIZA's aggregate liability for
any claim arising out of or related to the service is limited to the
amount of agency fees paid by you for the specific application giving
rise to the claim. VIZA is not liable for indirect, consequential, or
incidental damages, including missed travel, lost reservations,
visa-refusal consequences, or third-party portal outages.

## 8. Service availability

VIZA targets best-effort availability but does not commit to an SLA in
this draft. Government portals are out of VIZA's control; outages on
their side will pause submission attempts until the portal recovers.

## 9. Account suspension and termination

We may suspend or terminate an account if we have reasonable grounds
to believe it has been used to submit fraudulent or false information,
or that the account is being used to harm other users, our automation,
or third-party portals.

its conflict-of-laws principles. Disputes are subject to the exclusive
