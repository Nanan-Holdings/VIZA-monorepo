# US Non-Immigrant Visa (DS-160 B1/B2) — FAQ

## Documents required

- Passport valid at least 6 months past the intended date of entry.
- Photo 51×51 mm (600×600 px @ 300 dpi), white background, taken in
  the last 6 months — ICAO compliant.
- Travel itinerary, US contact (host or hotel), employment details
  for the last 5 years, education history.
- Social-media handles for the last 5 years (Form DS-160 §
  Additional Contact Information).

## Processing time

- DS-160 form completion via VIZA: ~30 minutes once you've supplied
  the canonical answer set.
- Embassy interview wait: country-dependent — check
  https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/us-visas/visa-information-resources/wait-times.html.
- The US visa-issuance time after a successful interview is typically
  3–7 business days.

## Refund eligibility

- Agency fee fully refundable until VIZA submits the DS-160.
- Once the DS-160 is submitted you get a confirmation page; you
  still pay the MRV fee separately on the consular bank link.
- The MRV fee is non-refundable per the US Department of State
  policy.

## Common rejection reasons

- Inconsistencies between DS-160 answers and prior US travel
  history (especially overstays).
- Photo not ICAO compliant — DS-160 photo upload is finicky.
- Inadequate evidence of ties to home country.
