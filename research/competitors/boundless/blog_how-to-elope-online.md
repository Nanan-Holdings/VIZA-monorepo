# How to Elope Online - A Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to get married without the hassle of a big wedding? Here are our expert tips on how to elope online and make it truly special.

Are you ready to tie the knot, but want a simpler alternative to a traditional ceremony? Eloping may be the perfect solution. No matter your situation or location, eloping can offer an intimate experience that allows you to truly connect with your partner and make this magical moment as special and unique as your love story. With modern technology, elopements have gotten even easier by allowing couples to [get married online](https://www.boundless.com/blog/proxy-marriage/) without having to leave the comfort of their home. Keep reading for step-by-step instructions on how to elope online.

## Eloping Online Benefits

One of the main advantages of eloping online is the convenience. Couples can choose their preferred location, time, and invitees without having to worry about coordinating travel for themselves or their guests. This can save a significant amount of time, money, and stress compared to traditional weddings.

Online elopements also offer more flexibility than traditional elopements. Couples can elope at any time, regardless of weather, location, or other potential obstacles. This opens up many creative opportunities, from incorporating virtual backgrounds to sharing the ceremony in real-time with friends and family from around the world.

## How to Elope Online

### Step 1: Choose Your Platform

The first step in planning an online elopement is choosing which platform will best suit your needs. There are many different platforms available, including video conferencing services like Zoom or Skype, streaming services like Twitch or YouTube Live, or even custom virtual wedding websites. Consider how many people you plan on inviting and whether or not they need access to certain features like chat rooms or polls. You'll also want to think about your budget and decide if you're willing to pay for any additional features that may be offered by premium services. Once you've chosen a platform, it's time to move onto step two.

### Step 2: Plan Your Ceremony

Once you've chosen a platform for your ceremony, it's time to start planning the details of the event itself! Start by deciding who will officiate your ceremony — will it be a family member? Or perhaps one of your close friends? You'll also want to pick out readings from literature or scripture, if desired. If possible, write out some vows yourself; this will make the occasion even more special. Lastly, consider adding any special touches such as music or photo slideshows that are meaningful for both of you.

### Step 3: Invite Guests

Now that all the details of your ceremony are in order, it's time to invite guests! Depending on which platform you choose (as well as how much money and time you have), there are various ways to do this. For example, if using Zoom or Skype, send out invitations with links directly via email. For YouTube Live or Twitch streams, create an event page with all relevant information and share it with friends and family members on social media platforms like Instagram and Facebook (just remember to include a link!).

Couples can make the online elopement process extremely simple by working with a service that will handle all the paperwork and coordination with government officials for you. For example,[Courtly](https://courtly.com/) can help you get legally married in under 24 hours from anywhere in the world. The whole application is done easily online, and just in case, they supply you with an expert concierge who will work with you to get your marriage license application processed as quickly as possible.

Eloping from home can be a memorable experience — and one that is completely tailored just for you two! With just a bit of preparation and planning ahead of time, hosting an online elopement can be stress-free while still giving all your loved ones the chance to join in on the celebration virtually. Have fun planning!
