A person seeking a [family-based green card](https://www.boundless.com/immigration-resources/complete-guide-to-family-based-immigration) can do so if they are a spouse, child, parent or sibling. A spouse seeking a [marriage-based green card](https://www.boundless.com/immigration-resources/marriage-based-green-cards-explained) must generally follow one of four different pathways, based on where they live and whether their spouse is a U.S. citizen or permanent resident (green card holder).

Boundless has produced a detailed guide for each pathway, so you can understand what to expect and then [navigate the immigration system with confidence](https://www.boundless.com/what-you-get-with-boundless-2/).

Alternatively, answering simple questions in [our app](https://explore.boundless.com/) will automatically present the correct [immigration forms](https://www.boundless.com/immigration-resources/uscis-gov-immigration-forms-and-fees) based on your answers.
