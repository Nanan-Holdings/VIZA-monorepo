## 3. What is SOC 2 Compliance?

SOC 2 (System and Organization Controls 2) is widely recognized as the gold standard for security compliance in the software-as-a-service (SaaS) industry. It requires companies to establish and adhere to stringent information security policies and procedures, encompassing three critical aspects of customer data management:

1.   Security
2.   Availability
3.   Confidentiality

Our SOC 2 Type II audit confirms that Boundless’s systems are meticulously designed to keep our clients’ sensitive data secure. This report verifies that we have consistently maintained processes and practices that meet or exceed the required levels of oversight and monitoring, allowing us to proactively identify and address any unusual activity.

## 4. Why SOC 2 Compliance Matters

The SOC 2 audit evaluates applicants across five key categories within the Trust Services Criteria established by the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants:

1.   **Security**: Verifying that our systems are protected against unauthorized access, use, or modification.
2.   **Availability**: Ensuring our systems are consistently available for operation and use.
3.   **Processing Integrity**: Confirming that our data processing is complete, valid, accurate, timely, and authorized.
4.   **Confidentiality**: Guaranteeing that information designated as confidential is rigorously protected.
5.   **Privacy**: Verifying that personal information is collected, used, retained, disclosed, and disposed of in line with our commitments and requirements.
