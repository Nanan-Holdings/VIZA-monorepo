### Why Choose the EB-1A Visa?

With an EB-1A visa, you gain more than a green card—you gain independence, flexibility, and recognition for your extraordinary ability.

![Image 7: The icon of a page of paper symbolizing files](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/689e09427059afed9bd7b4a5/68a5c116919ee34692909c9d_file.svg)

Allows you to live and work permanently in the U.S. based on your extraordinary ability

![Image 8: The icon of a page of paper symbolizing files](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/689e09427059afed9bd7b4a5/68a5c116919ee34692909c9d_file.svg)

**No job offer or employer sponsorship is required**, giving you the freedom to work for any company or start your own business

![Image 9: The icon of a page of paper symbolizing files](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/689e09427059afed9bd7b4a5/68a5c116919ee34692909c9d_file.svg)

Direct path to a green card without the need for labor certification

![Image 10: The icon of a page of paper symbolizing files](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/689e09427059afed9bd7b4a5/68a5c116919ee34692909c9d_file.svg)

Priority processing with typically shorter wait times than other employment-based categories

![Image 11: The icon of a page of paper symbolizing files](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/689e09427059afed9bd7b4a5/68a5c116919ee34692909c9d_file.svg)

Spouses and unmarried children under 21 can be included in your application for permanent residency

![Image 12: The icon of a page of paper symbolizing files](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/689e09427059afed9bd7b4a5/68a5c116919ee34692909c9d_file.svg)

No degree requirements, making it accessible to self-taught experts and innovators

![Image 13: The icon of a page of paper symbolizing files](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/689e09427059afed9bd7b4a5/68a5c116919ee34692909c9d_file.svg)

Flexibility to work across different projects or ventures in your field of expertise

### Got Questions? We’re On It

This is some text inside of a div block.

What is the EB-1A visa?

The EB-1A is a green card for individuals who demonstrate extraordinary ability in their field, such as the sciences, arts, business, education, or athletics.

Do I need a job offer to apply?

**No.** The EB-1A is one of the few green card types that does not require a U.S. employer to sponsor you.

How do I know if I qualify?

Start with our free screening. If eligible, our legal team will assess your strongest criteria and guide you through building your case.

What makes Boundless different?

We combine attorney strategy with end-to-end case preparation—without the complexity of traditional law firms or DIY platforms.

 Can my family be included?

Yes. Your spouse and unmarried children under 21 can be included in your EB-1A petition for permanent residency.
