To ship your application, complete at least two-thirds of your payments.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Got Questions? We’re On It

‍

Is there a minimum payment due before my application is shipped?

We require a minimum number of payments to ensure your application is complete and ready for submission. This allows us to dedicate the necessary resources to prepare your case thoroughly and maximize your chances of success.

Before we can submit your petition, your account must be at least **two-thirds (2/3) paid**.

**What does this mean for you?**

If you're on an installment plan and your petition is ready before you've paid off two-thirds of your balance, you'll need to make a one-time "catch-up" payment to reach that amount before your application can be shipped.

**Here’s a quick example:**

*   You choose a **12-month plan** for a $1,200 service ($100/month).
*   Your petition is ready to go in **Month 3**. You've paid $300 so far.
*   The two-thirds (2/3) requirement is **$800**.
*   You would need to make a **$500 catch-up payment** before we can submit it.
*   After that, you'd just have **4 monthly payments of $100 left**.

This policy allows us to begin work right away while giving you the flexibility of monthly payments.

What is an “installment”?

An “installment” is one of several equal payments made over a fixed period of time, such as 6 months.

What payment methods can I use?

You may use a Visa, MasterCard, American Express, or Discover card with a payment plan. Recurly can accept a credit card, debit card, or prepaid debit card.

How does Boundless determine when to ship my application?

A well-prepared, accurate application is your best chance for success. Our shipping policy ensures several quality checks, including an attorney review (Premium service), are completed before submission. We ship upon your approval of the final review AND after receiving the minimum payments according to your chosen plan.

What’s the minimum number of payments required before shipping?

Before we can submit your petition, your account must be at least **two-thirds (2/3) paid.**For more questions, contact our customer support team at payment-solutions@boundless.com.

How do I update my billing or personal information for my payment plan?

Contact our Customer Support Team at [payment-solutions@boundless.com](mailto:payment-solutions@boundless.com) to update your billing or personal information for your payment plan.

When do the payments start?

Your first payment will be charged the day you select a payment plan. Recurly will then process each future payment on the same date of every following month until the balance is paid off. For example, if the first installment appeared on your credit-card or checking-account statement on the 5th of the month, all future payments will also appear on your statement on the 5th of each following month.

Recurly will also send you a confirmation email each month after they charge your card, so you can keep track of your payments.

If I choose a payment plan, when can I start the Boundless process?

You can start on your application as soon as you select a payment option with Boundless. Opting to pay over time with one of our payment plans will never halt your progress, and our team will get to work on your application as soon as you sign up.

Are there payment plan options available for Boundless Essential and Premium?

Yes! You can opt for a payment plan whether you choose the Essential application package or the Premium application package. You can also choose to include your government fees with either application package.

What if I switch from the Essential service to the Premium service?

You can switch from the Essential service to Premium (with lawyer support) at any time during the Boundless process. If you choose to switch, you will be asked to pay the difference between the Essential and Premium service price in a one-time payment. Your installment plan will be unchanged.

Is there a credit check?

Neither Boundless nor Recurly will check your credit, including your credit history and credit score. Signing up for a payment plan will not affect your credit.

Why is there no interest?

You will not be charged any interest on any of your installments — including any amount held — for the entire duration of your payment plan.

Please note that if you choose to pay your Boundless fee using a payment plan, your base cost will be higher than if you choose to pay in full. In addition, if you choose to pay your USCIS fees over time using Recurly’s payment plan, a service fee will be included in your total cost. The service fee included in your total cost is to account for our team writing the check to USCIS on your behalf. To see the exact service fee for your specific application and your total costs, please explore the checkout page or reach out to our team at help@boundless.com.

Do you offer any discounts?

If you choose to work with Boundless on your K-1 visa application, we make it easy to transition to the next phase in your immigration journey – applying for a marriage green card. Returning Boundless customers get 12% off the marriage green card service. We’ll even track and safely store your K-1 forms and supporting documents so you don’t have to start your green card application from scratch.

Are government fees covered by the payment plan?

Yes! You can also opt to simplify the entire payment process and pay the government fees on your payment plan. Boundless will write the check for you when it comes time to file with the government, eliminating any risk of payment mistakes which can cause delays. You can explore all of your payment options and calculate your exact government fees at checkout.

Please note that if you choose to pay your Boundless fee using a payment plan, your base cost will be higher than if you choose to pay in full. In addition, if you choose to pay your USCIS fees over time using Recurly’s payment plan, a service fee will be included in your total cost. The service fee included in your total cost is to account for our team writing the check to USCIS on your behalf. To see the exact service fee for your specific application and your total costs, please explore the checkout page or reach out to our team at help@boundless.com.

What if I decide I want to include government fees on my Boundless application at a later time?

You can pay for government fees later even after checkout and you’ve already paid using a payment plan. Any government fees paid later will be processed as a separate charge and will not modify your existing payment plan. For questions about paying for government fees later, please reach out to our team for help at [support.team@boundless.com](mailto:support.team@boundless.com).

What if I no longer wish to use Boundless and want a refund?

Every Boundless customer is protected by our 14-day money back guarantee. If you’re not satisfied with Boundless or your immigration plans change within the first 14 days of becoming a customer, we’ll issue a full refund of all your past payments, and you will not be responsible for any future payments.
