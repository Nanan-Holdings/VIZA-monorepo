# We're Acquiring Bridge to Transform Global Immigration for Businesses and Families

We’re excited to announce our acquisition of Bridge, a leader in simplifying U.S. immigration and creating immigration programs for businesses and their global teams.

![Image 6](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/68a3050860dd863f875cffa9/69164631b1ad87eda3ce3c67_68c37a3a40d42bb51e705b6c_BL.jpeg)

As the world becomes increasingly connected and the hunt for talent transcends borders, the need for seamless immigration solutions is more important than ever. At Boundless, our mission has always been to help individuals and families easily and affordably navigate the complex immigration process. Now, with the addition of Bridge, we are helping workers, businesses, and entrepreneurs on their immigration journeys as well.

Together, we provide tools to help companies not only manage the work visa process but also build their hiring and corporate strategies around it. By streamlining the immigration process, we help companies fill priority roles faster, improve employee retention, and reduce the load on HR teams. Boundless, now with Bridge, makes it simple for companies to access and hire the best talent in the world.

## How is what we’re doing different from a law firm?

**We’re immigration specialists**

Countless law firms offer immigration “support” but [only 3% of all lawyers](https://www.ibisworld.com/industry-statistics/employment/immigration-lawyers-attorneys-united-states/#:~:text=There%20are%2014%2C601%20people%20employed,the%20US%20as%20of%202023) in the U.S. specialize in immigration.

Boundless and Bridge have more than a decade of experience in helping companies and families navigate the U.S. immigration system, with a nearly 100% success rate on our applications

**We look at the big picture**

Most law firms that specialize in immigration law only offer select services in specific categories (e.g., family visa or work visa, not both) and don’t necessarily strategize with clients on the big picture for their entire immigration journey.

Boundless and Bridge are different. We understand the intersection of life and work, and we help employers, entrepreneurs, and immigrants build their businesses and their lives in the U.S. With more than 500 corporate clients served, and over 150,000 successful applications filed, Boundless and Bridge offer more expertise and a higher rate of success than any single law firm, and because we’re an online company, we can help our customers and clients wherever they are.

**We’re affordable, accessible, and inclusive**

Nearly 85% of immigration lawyers are concentrated in large cities like New York, Los Angeles, or Chicago, and charge tens of thousands of dollars per client, depending on the complexity of their case and their immigration goals.

Our technology allows customers and clients to partner with us wherever they are and in every time zone. We not only streamline our communication, but also the costs and immigration benefits for our customers and clients, allowing businesses to build and scale their immigration programs and benefits on their own timeline.

Moreover, we know that immigration is never a straight path, in one narrow category - which is why our services cover a suite of solutions to support businesses, individuals, and families.

**Empowering businesses to bridge the global skills gap**

At Boundless, we know that work and family visas are intertwined. By teaming up with Bridge, we’re able to offer solutions that are not just about immigration - they are about _potential_.

We now empower companies to:

*   **Access Global Talent Pools**: With expedited and streamlined visa processing, your company can hire without immigration status constraints.
*   **Enhance Employee Experience**: Offering a seamless immigration experience to your international workforce positively impacts satisfaction and retention.
*   **Ensure Compliance & Gain Insights**: Bridge’s compliance and reporting features will ensure that your company remains in line with legal requirements while providing data-driven insights for decision-making.
*   **Strengthen Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Strategies**: By facilitating a more inclusive workforce, we help businesses build a foundation of diversity that fuels innovation and growth.

**Helping Entrepreneurs Drive Innovation in the U.S.**

We aren’t just about helping companies hire the workers they need. With Bridge, we also help entrepreneurs come to the U.S. to realize their dreams, and that’s a win for everyone. We know that immigrant entrepreneurs are huge economic drivers — more than half of America’s startups valued at $1 billion or more were [founded by immigrants](https://nfap.com/research/new-nfap-policy-brief-immigrant-entrepreneurs-and-u-s-billion-dollar-companies/) and Fortune 500 companies founded by immigrants or their children [employ nearly 15 million worldwide](https://www.americanimmigrationcouncil.org/news/new-american-fortune-500-report-reveals-impact-immigrant-entrepreneurship). With our help, entrepreneurs can focus on their ideas, while we take care of the rest.

**And There's More: Supporting Families Too!**

We recognize that for many international workers, family is an integral part of the immigration journey. Boundless will continue to provide unparalleled family visa services, and together with Bridge, we will now support companies in building comprehensive immigration programs that consider both work-related and family-based visas.

## Our Vision Moving Forward

By integrating Bridge's exceptional team and cutting-edge platform, Boundless is building an ecosystem where immigration processes become immigration strategies for families and businesses. Whether it’s supporting an applicant with a travel visa for an interview, supporting the transfer of an H-1B, or shifting from an H-1B to a marriage green card – we’re the first company to recognize that the global workforce is only as effective as the families that drive them and comprehensive immigration strategies are paramount to innovation in the U.S.

We welcome Bridge to Boundless as we chart this transformative journey together. Whether you’re an individual, a family, or a business, join us as we redefine what’s possible in immigration.

Discover more about how Boundless Immigration and Bridge can [revolutionize your immigration journey](https://www.boundless.com/lp/boundlessxbridge/) and read the press release [here](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20230822865632/en/Boundless-Immigration-Acquires-Bridge-to-Transform-Immigration-for-Businesses-and-Families).
